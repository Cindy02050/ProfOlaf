Snowball Sampling Start

One-Time Setup

This process initializes your snowball sampling by searching Google Scholar for your initial paper titles and storing them in the database. This should only be run once at the beginning of your research.

What This Does
  • Reads paper titles from your input file
  • Searches Google Scholar for each paper
  • Extracts citation information and metadata
  • Stores the initial papers in the database
  • Creates the foundation for snowball sampling
